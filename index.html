Welcome

Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Mark, and I am a seasoned Web Developer based in the vibrant city of Edinburgh. With over 20 years of experience, I have worked in various web development roles across the globe, from the Shetland Islands to Sydney, Australia.

I created this website as a platform to showcase some of the highlights of my work over the years. Here, you can explore some of the exciting projects I have been involved in, as well as gain insight into my skills, expertise, and approach to web development. Thank you for visiting, and please feel free to reach out to me if you have any questions or inquiries.

One thing that has remained constant throughout all my previous positions has always been my desire to make my work accessible to everyone regardless of OS or device. I have created this site so it is fully responsive and can be viewed equally well on any platform.
