Visit Scotland Work

Map Box Demo

Below is a demo of a Mapbox map of ferry routes to the islands of Scotland that I incorporated into the map via a GEO layer that pulls in JSON data from an external API. I made this as a demonstration of how Visit Scotland could incorporate Mapbox maps as a dev-module in their Alterian CMS. Mapbox is really cool you can get a 3d view of the map by right mouse clicking and moving up and down over the map.
